Welcome to TuteeNep!

Supporting the Academic Journey of High School Students

At TuteeNep, we are passionate about helping Nepali students reach their full academic potential and achieve their learning goals.

Our Mission

Our mission at TuteeNep is simple: to provide quality tutoring services that empower students to excel academically and thrive in their educational journey. We believe that every student deserves access to personalized support and guidance to help them succeed.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our tutoring services to the impact we make on students' lives.

Inclusivity

We believe in creating an inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued, supported, and respected.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and continually seek new ways to enhance the learning experience and meet the evolving needs of students.

Basic Functionality

  1. Tutors sign up on our website, sharing their education and teaching background.
  2. Students can view tutor profiles on our site.
  3. Students can easily find and contact tutors in their area.
